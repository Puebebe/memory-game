  • This game uses mouse only
  • Use your left mouse button to click on elements
  • To start your game:
    1. Click "Start Game" button
    2. Click one of the puzzle images
    3. Remember image that's appeared to you
    4. Click next image
    5. If they're the same, they will last appeared to you to the end of game
    6. If they're different, both images will disapear and the puzzle image will show up on both places
    7. Repeat steps 2-4 until game ends by matching all of images
